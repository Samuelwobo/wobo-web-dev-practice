ABOUT WOBO HOTEL

The Limewood Hotel is a classy, modern boutique hotel with 40 air-conditioned rooms offering our guest all the comfort of a luxury hotel, with high-end services provided by a professionally trained team, in the reserved area of Port Harcourt metropolis.
Nestled on the corner of Sani Abacha road is a beautiful neoclassical building, the balconies on the 3rd and 5th floors give the guests an unobstructed view of the city and an envious external architectural structure.
Wobo Hotel allows visitors to mix business with pleasure. Ideally located in the buzzing and luminous street in the heart of the famous Government Reserved Area Phase 3, The Wobo Hotel is a very unique spot, gathering all the best places: high fashion boutiques, designer fragrance shops, jewelry stores, restaurants, night clubs, polo club, cinemas and more.
We are also 25 minutes’ drive from the international airport, 2 minutes’ drive from the nearest police post, 10 minutes’ drive from the closest fire service station, 10 minutes from the air force and army base. Our central location gives our guests the logistics advantage to get business done faster via the accessible front and back road network to the entire city.
We aim to cater to each guests unique taste through our professionally tailored service and uniquely designed rooms. Each room welcomes you into a world of in-room entertainment complete with the latest technology in IPTV, Video on Demand and BYOD, giving our guest the freedom to watch what they want and when they want to watch it. We urge you to step into our world of comfort and relaxation in any of our contemporarily designed rooms, consisting of elements carefully curated to showcase an embodiment of high end hospitality experience. Simply put, “there is no hotel like this in Port Harcourt”
Wobo hotel also features a rooftop swimming pool, classy gym and a spa offering aromatherapy. These features enhances delivery of distinct experiences for our guests. In addition to the custom designed rooms, our hotel features the sensational excellente nourriture “Le Tycoon” continental restaurant, gallery bar, exclusive penthouse lounge for guests to relax, unwind and purpose built meeting room for corporate meetings and informal receptions. Guests also have access to a comprehensive in-room dining menu courtesy of the restaurant.
We offer the best mix of culture and the highest standards of hospitality. All of which reflects in our offerings of luxurious accommodation, exceptional guest experience, and superior security provision.
Wobo Hotel welcomes you on this journey where you walk in as a guest and leave as part of a fulfilled family.
